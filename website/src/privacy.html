Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 17, 2026
Last Updated: April 17, 2026
Developer: Stone Bicycle Coalition LLC
Contact: stonebicyclecoalition@gmail.com

Overview

Stone Bicycle Coalition ("the App") is a community cycling app developed by Stone Bicycle Coalition LLC. Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

The short version: Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or sell any personal information.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

  • Ride recordings — GPS tracks, distance, elevation, and duration from rides you record
  • Expedition journal entries — photos, notes, audio, and GPS data you capture during rides
  • User preferences — app settings, onboarding state, favorite routes
  • Ride history — session counts, total miles, and season statistics

This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. We do not operate servers that receive data from the App.

Permissions the App Requests

Location (Optional)

  • Used to record GPS tracks during rides and show your position on route maps
  • Location data is stored only on your device
  • You can deny location permission and still browse routes, bikes, events, and community content
  • Background location is used only during active ride recording with a visible notification

Camera (Optional)

  • Used to capture photos for expedition journal entries
  • Photos are stored locally on your device
  • You can deny camera permission and still use all other app features

Nearby Devices / Local Network (Rally Radio)

  • Rally Radio uses peer-to-peer connectivity for push-to-talk voice chat during group rides
  • No data passes through any server — communication is device-to-device only
  • No accounts or identities are created

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No device identifiers or fingerprinting
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No crash reporting to external services
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No cookies or web tracking
  • No social media data

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

The only external connections are:

  • MapLibre / OpenStreetMap tiles — map tiles are loaded from public tile servers when viewing route maps. No personal data is sent.
  • WordPress REST API (optional) — the App may sync community posts and events from stonebicyclecoalition.com. No personal data is sent.
  • App Store / Google Play — for download and updates (managed by Apple/Google)

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal data with any third party.

Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all App data at any time:

  • iOS: Settings > General > iPhone Storage > StoneBC > Delete App
  • Android: Settings > Apps > Stone Bicycle Coalition > Storage > Clear Data

When you delete the App, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. The App contains no user accounts, no social features requiring personal data, and no data collection mechanisms that could inadvertently capture children's data.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version at this URL and update the "Last Updated" date. Material changes will be noted in the App's release notes.
