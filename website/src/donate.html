🚲 Donate a Bike

Your old bike can become someone's reliable transportation. We accept bikes in any condition!

We accept:

  • Bikes of all sizes and types
  • Bikes in any condition (even non-working)
  • Bike parts and accessories
  • Helmets (good condition only)
  • Locks, lights, pumps, tools

Drop-off location:

925 9th Street #3
Rapid City, SD 57701

During open shop hours or by appointment

Where Your Donation Goes

🏅

Earn-A-Bike

$75 provides a refurbished bike, helmet, and lock to one graduate.

🔧

Tools & Parts

$100 stocks essential parts like tubes, tires, and cables for a month.

📚

Safety Education

$50 provides helmets and materials for a youth safety class.

🏠

Operations

$250 supports one month of open shop hours for the community.

Corporate & Foundation Support

We welcome partnerships with businesses and foundations who share our mission of building community through cycling.

Benefits include recognition on our website and in our shop, involvement in community events, and the satisfaction of supporting accessible transportation.
