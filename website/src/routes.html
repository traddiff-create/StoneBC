How to Use GPX Files

GPX files can be loaded into most cycling computers and smartphone apps:

Garmin

Upload via Garmin Connect or copy directly to device.

Wahoo

Sync through Wahoo app or send directly to device.

Strava

Upload GPX and create a route for turn-by-turn.

Ride with GPS

Import GPX and sync to any device.
