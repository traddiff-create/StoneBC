Building Community Through Cycling

We're creating an open-source bicycle cooperative model in Rapid City, South Dakota. Learn to repair bikes, earn your own bicycle, and join a community of cyclists.

Our Programs Support Us

Our Mission

Create a replicable, open-source bicycle cooperative model that empowers communities to establish their own bike co-ops. We make bicycle repair, education, and ownership accessible to all.

💡

Community Ownership

Build a bicycle cooperative where cyclists own and operate the space together.

🔗

Open Source

Document everything so any community can adapt and replicate under their local laws.

🚲

Accessible Cycling

Make bicycle repair, education, and ownership accessible regardless of income.
