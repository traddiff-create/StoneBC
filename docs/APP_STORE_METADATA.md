## Description

Stone Bicycle Coalition connects cyclists in your community. Browse refurbished bikes, discover local routes, stay updated on events, and talk hands-free with your riding group — all in one app.

**The Quarry Marketplace**
Find your next ride. Browse refurbished bikes with detailed specs, pricing, and condition reports. Filter by type — road, hybrid, mountain, cruiser — and reach out when you find your match.

**42 Black Hills Routes**
Explore cycling routes across the Black Hills with interactive maps, elevation profiles, and difficulty ratings. From easy creek paths to expert mountain trails.

**Rally Radio**
Talk to your riding group hands-free. Push-to-talk voice chat works peer-to-peer — no cell service needed. Perfect for group rides in remote areas.

**Community Feed**
Stay in the loop with events, workshops, and announcements from your local bike co-op. Open shop hours, group rides, volunteer opportunities — it's all here.

Built by Stone Bicycle Coalition in Rapid City, South Dakota. Open source — any bike co-op can fork and customize this app for their community.
