Overview

The legendary 8 Over 7 as a 3-day bikepacking trip. Depart Spearfish Friday, ride Mickelson Trail to Sylvan Lake to camp. Saturday and Sunday cover the full 100+ mile singletrack link-up through pine forest, high-country ridgelines, and technical terrain — with resupply stops in Custer and Deerfield. Back in Spearfish by Sunday evening.

Late Join Option

Can't make Friday? Get dropped off at Sylvan Lake Friday afternoon/evening to meet the group. Easy to catch up for the main event on Saturday.

Tour Notes

If weather looks terrible, postpone to May 22–24.

This is bikepacking — pack overnight gear for Sylvan Lake.

Carry enough water and food between resupply points. Cell service is spotty.

The full route climbs nearly 10,000 feet across 3 days. Pace yourself.

Trail conditions vary — check recent reports for deadfall and washouts.
