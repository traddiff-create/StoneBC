Stone Bicycle Coalition is your cycling companion for the Black Hills. Browse routes, find bikes, join community rides, and connect with fellow riders -- all in one app.

ROUTES
Explore 42 cycling routes across the Black Hills with interactive maps, elevation profiles, and turn-by-turn details. Filter by difficulty, distance, and surface type. Download routes for offline use on your next adventure.

THE QUARRY - BIKE MARKETPLACE
Browse available bikes from the coalition's inventory. Filter by type, status, and price. Find your next ride or donate a bike to the community.

RALLY RADIO
Push-to-talk voice chat for group rides. No cell service required -- Rally Radio uses peer-to-peer connectivity so your group stays connected even in remote areas. No accounts, no servers, no internet needed.

COMMUNITY FEED
Stay up to date with coalition news, events, and ride reports. Read about upcoming community rides, workshops, and advocacy initiatives.

EVENTS & PROGRAMS
Find upcoming rides, workshops, and community events. From Pedal for Empathy to bikepacking tours, there's always something happening.

TOUR GUIDES
Multi-day ride guides with day-by-day itineraries, stop timelines, and ride recording checklists. Plan your Black Hills bikepacking adventure with the Brewvet brewery tour or the 8 Over 7 expedition.

EXPEDITION JOURNAL
Document your rides like Lewis and Clark. Capture photos, notes, audio, and GPS tracks during multi-day expeditions. Collaborate with your riding group and export beautiful HTML reports.

BUILT FOR THE BLACK HILLS
Stone Bicycle Coalition is a grassroots community organization in Rapid City, South Dakota, dedicated to advancing active transportation and building a more connected region through cycling. Founded by Rory and Nicole Stone.

FREE & OPEN SOURCE
No accounts. No ads. No tracking. No subscriptions. The app is free and the source code is open for other bike co-ops to fork and customize for their own communities.
