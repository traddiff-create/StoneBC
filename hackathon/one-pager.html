The Problem

Rapid City's cycling community is fragmented. No central hub for routes, rides, advocacy, or affordable bike access. New cyclists don't know where to start.

The Solution

Community Moments is a mobile app connecting people through motion and education. Already live in TestFlight with:

  • 56 GPS routes with elevation profiles & difficulty ratings
  • Rally Radio — peer-to-peer voice chat (no cell needed)
  • The Quarry — affordable bike marketplace
  • Community feed with events & news
  • Apple Watch haptic guidance
  • Works fully offline

Weekend Build Plan

  1. AI Route Advisor — "What kind of ride?" → personalized recommendation
  2. Ride Safety Overlay — real-time weather on route maps
