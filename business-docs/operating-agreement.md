## ARTICLE IV — MANAGEMENT

4.1 **Manager-Managed.** The Company shall be manager-managed. Rory J. Stone is designated as the initial Manager.

4.2 **Authority.** The Manager shall have authority to conduct the ordinary business of the Company, including opening and managing bank accounts, entering contracts, and making day-to-day operational decisions.

4.3 **Major Decisions.** The following require unanimous Member consent:
- (a) Admitting new Members
- (b) Selling all or substantially all Company assets
- (c) Dissolving the Company
- (d) Incurring debt exceeding $5,000
- (e) Amending this Operating Agreement

4.4 **Compensation.** The Manager shall not receive compensation solely for management duties unless agreed to by all Members.

---

## ARTICLE V — ALLOCATIONS AND DISTRIBUTIONS

5.1 **Profits and Losses.** Net profits and losses shall be allocated to the Members in proportion to their Ownership Interests (50/50).

5.2 **Distributions.** Distributions shall be made at the discretion of the Manager, in proportion to Ownership Interests. No distribution shall be made that would render the Company unable to pay its debts as they become due.

5.3 **Tax Allocations.** For federal income tax purposes, the Company shall be treated as a partnership. Tax items shall be allocated in accordance with the Members' Ownership Interests and Section 704(b) of the Internal Revenue Code.

---

## ARTICLE VI — TAX MATTERS

6.1 **Tax Classification.** The Company elects to be classified as a partnership for federal income tax purposes.

6.2 **Tax Returns.** The Manager shall cause the Company to file all required federal, state, and local tax returns (Form 1065) and shall furnish each Member with a Schedule K-1 annually.

6.3 **Tax Matters Partner.** Rory J. Stone is designated as the Partnership Representative (formerly Tax Matters Partner) under Section 6223 of the Internal Revenue Code.

6.4 **Fiscal Year.** The Company's fiscal year is the calendar year (January 1 through December 31).

---

## ARTICLE VII — TRANSFER OF INTERESTS

7.1 **Restrictions.** No Member may transfer their Ownership Interest without the prior written consent of all other Members.

7.2 **Right of First Refusal.** If a Member wishes to transfer their interest, the remaining Members shall have a right of first refusal to purchase the interest at fair market value.

---

## ARTICLE VIII — DISSOLUTION

8.1 **Events of Dissolution.** The Company shall dissolve upon:
- (a) Unanimous written consent of all Members
- (b) Entry of a judicial decree of dissolution
- (c) Any event that makes it unlawful for the Company to continue

8.2 **Winding Up.** Upon dissolution, the Manager shall wind up Company affairs, pay debts, and distribute remaining assets to Members in proportion to their Ownership Interests.

---

## ARTICLE IX — GENERAL PROVISIONS

9.1 **Governing Law.** This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of South Dakota.

9.2 **Amendments.** This Agreement may be amended only by unanimous written consent of all Members.

9.3 **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement among the Members and supersedes all prior agreements.

9.4 **Severability.** If any provision is held invalid, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force.
