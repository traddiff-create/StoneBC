## Key Argument for More Funding

- Rapid City has **no established bike advocacy events** — this fills a vacuum
- SBC has the **infrastructure** (LLC, EIN, partners, website, community network)
- Higher funding = **more community impact per dollar** (fixed costs are already covered at $1K)
- The food bank donation scales with the grant — **AHA's money does double duty**
- A well-funded event creates **recurring momentum** — not just a one-off
