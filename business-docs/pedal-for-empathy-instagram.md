## VISUAL STYLE GUIDE FOR ALL POSTS

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Bright emerald green (bikes, nature, forward motion)
- Secondary: Warm gold/amber (community, warmth, gathering)
- Accent: Sky blue (Rapid City sky, openness, freedom)
- Neutral: White, light gray (cleanliness, clarity)

**Photography Style:**
- Real action (people actually riding, actually cleaning)
- Diverse faces and body types represented
- Genuine smiles, not staged
- Include the Rapid City skyline/landmarks when possible
- Mix wide shots (community scale) with close-ups (individual moments)
- Capture the before-and-after of cleanup areas

**Typography:**
- Bold, readable sans-serif for all text overlays
- Keep text centered or follow rule of thirds
- High contrast for accessibility (dark text on light, light text on dark)
- Keep text to 3-4 lines maximum per story or carousel slide

**Tone Across All Posts:**
- Warm, inviting, neighbor-to-neighbor
- Action-oriented ("let's ride," "we show up," "join us")
- Grateful (always thank partners and participants)
- Inclusive (always mention "all experience levels," "families welcome," "no drop-offs")
- Hopeful (this ride is part of something bigger)
- Never corporate, never sterile, never top-down
