## Project Overview

### Project Title
Pedal for Empathy: A Community Ride & Cleanup for Rapid City Streets

### Project Summary
Stone Bicycle Coalition will host a community bike ride and street/trail cleanup event on May 2, 2026, in Rapid City, SD. The event will bring together cyclists, neighbors, families, and local partners to clean up urban bike corridors and public spaces, advocate for safer and more equitable access to streets and green space, and celebrate the power of community action. The event is open to all Rapid City residents — no experience required.

## The Empathy Angle

In Rapid City, access to safe, clean public streets and bike infrastructure is not evenly distributed. Many neighborhoods lack safe cycling routes, and shared public spaces often go unmaintained. This creates invisible barriers — people who would otherwise ride, walk, or gather outdoors simply don't, and community connection suffers.

Pedal for Empathy addresses three intersecting needs:

- **Equitable access:** Advocating for streets and bike infrastructure that serve everyone, not just experienced cyclists or well-resourced neighborhoods.
- **Environmental stewardship:** Tangibly improving shared public spaces through hands-on cleanup.
- **Community connection:** Bringing people together across backgrounds for a shared purpose — demonstrating that neighbors still show up for each other.

Cycling is one of the most democratic forms of transportation — affordable, accessible, and human-scale. When we make our streets cleaner and safer for people on bikes, we make them better for everyone. That is empathy in action.

## Project Plan

### Event Format
- Morning community ride along urban bike corridors in Rapid City (all paces welcome, family-friendly)
- Post-ride cleanup of bike lanes, greenways, and public spaces along the route
- Community gathering with snacks and information on local bike advocacy efforts
- Photo documentation and social media outreach to amplify the day's message

### Local Partners
- **City of Rapid City Parks & Recreation** — coordination for cleanup permits, trash/recycling collection, and route support
- **Local bike shops** — promotion to their customer base, potential donation of cleanup gloves/supplies, and volunteer mobilization

## About Stone Bicycle Coalition

Stone Bicycle Coalition is a community bike advocacy organization based in Rapid City, South Dakota. Our mission is to make Rapid City more bikeable, connected, and livable for everyone — from seasoned cyclists to families getting on a bike for the first time. We work with city partners, local businesses, and community members to improve bike infrastructure, promote active transportation, and build community through cycling.

While we are a small but active organization, we have an engaged local network and established relationships with City Parks & Recreation and local cycling businesses. Pedal for Empathy represents exactly the kind of tangible, community-first action we believe Rapid City needs.
