# Pedal for Empathy — Website Announcement

## For stonebicyclecoalition.com (WordPress)

### Post Title
Stone Bicycle Coalition Awarded American Empathy Project Grant

### Post Body

Stone Bicycle Coalition has been selected for a grant from the American Empathy Project, a national initiative by the American Humanist Association. We were chosen from over 600 applications across the country.

The grant supports our upcoming event: **Pedal for Empathy: A Community Ride & Cleanup for Rapid City Streets**.

**When:** Saturday, May 2, 2026
**Where:** Rapid City bike path and The Gap
**Cost:** Free — open to everyone

Join us for a family-friendly community bike ride followed by a hands-on cleanup of bike lanes, greenways, and public spaces along the route. All paces welcome — no experience required. After the ride, stick around for snacks and community.

A portion of the grant will be donated to Feeding South Dakota to support food security in our community.

**Partners:** City of Rapid City Parks & Recreation, Acme Bikes

**Want to volunteer?** Follow us on Instagram or reach out at stonebicyclecoalition@gmail.com.

Together, we're showing that empathy starts with showing up for your neighbors — on two wheels.
