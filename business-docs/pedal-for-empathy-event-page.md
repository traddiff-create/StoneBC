## WHAT TO EXPECT SECTION

### The Experience

**Morning Community Ride**
We'll pedal together through Rapid City's urban bike corridors—the streets our community uses every day. This is a casual, social pace ride. We're not racing; we're connecting. You'll see familiar routes through fresh eyes and meet your neighbors who share a passion for biking and community.

**Street Cleanup**
After the ride, we'll roll up our sleeves and clean up the streets we just rode on. Grab a grabber, fill a bag, and help make our city streets cleaner and more inviting for everyone who uses them—cyclists and pedestrians alike.

**Community Gathering**
We'll wrap up with food, drinks, and a chance to hang out. Meet local bike shop owners, chat with Parks & Rec staff about Rapid City's bike infrastructure, and celebrate what we've accomplished together.
